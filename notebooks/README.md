This folder contains the scripts to reproduce the figures in 'The impact of waveform systematics and Gaussian noise on the interpretation ofGW231123':

1. 'fig1 systematics': corner plot GW231123 total mass and mass ratio for five waveform models. Samples from the LVK release [zenodo](https://zenodo.org/records/17437902)
2. 'plot_posterior_waveform.py': (figure 1 right panel). From bilby PE samples, plot maximum-likelihood and 90% credible interval waveform interval for two waveform models
2. 'fig2_GW231123_zeronoise.ipynb' corner plots comparing GW231123 and NRSur maximum-likelihood waveform simulation in zero-noise
3. 'fig3_5_impact_gaussian_noise.ipynb' primary and secondary mass, spins magnitude, effective and precessing spin posterior distribution for GW231123, NRSur maximum-likelihood simulation in zero-noise and in 20 Gaussian noise realizations. (Figures 3, 4, 5, 8)
4. 'fig6_JS_waveform_systematic.ipynb' Jensen–Shannon divergence between NRSur and XPHM, and NRSur and XO4a posterior distributions
5. 'fig7_corner_plot.ipynb' GW231123 corner plot
6. 'fig9_H_L_JS.ipynb' Jensen–Shannon divergence between LIGO Hanford-only and LIGO Livingston-only inference
7. 'fig10_LIGOAsharp.ipynb' comparison of the power spectral density, and source properties inference for GW231123 and with the expected sensitivity for LIGO A#
